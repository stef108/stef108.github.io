Questions

  1. How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?
  2. How can you scan a document a send it to your laptop at HZ?
  3. What do you need to do when you are sick / show symptoms of coronavirus?
  4. How can you book a project space in one of the wings?
  5. What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

Answers

  1. By sending your file to the printer, going to the printer and check it out with your touch-id.
  2. Log yourself in with your touch-id, then choose the option scan. Press scan and send, Send to me, Yes, Start.
  3. Send a message through teams or call.
  4. Go to selfservice portal, reservations and visitors, then just follow the steps there.
  5. Go to selfservice portal, reservations and visitors, then pick car instead of room.
